Chicken Manchurian

Chicken Manchurian is not an authentic Chinese dish. But is a fusion food from the Indian Chinese cuisine which is developed with the adaptation of Chinese seasoning & cooking methods to suit Indian taste buds.

Tandoori Chicken

Tandoori chicken is a chicken dish prepared by roasting chicken marinated in yogurt and spices in a tandoor, a cylindrical clay oven. The dish originated from the Indian subcontinent and is popular in many other parts of the world.

Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a savory chicken and rice dish that includes layers of chicken, rice, and aromatics that are steamed together. ... When fried, the spices and aromatics on the exterior of the chicken caramelize into an insanely flavorful crust.

Chicken Seekh Kabab

Kebabs are famous all over the world for all the right reasons. These are not only delicious and easy to eat but are also full of health, especially if you make them the ‘healthy way’.

Chicken Dry Fry Boneless

Chicken Dry Fry is a Chinese recipe which is prepared with chicken, cornflour, egg whites and a combination of spices. This easy, non-vegetarian dish treats your taste buds with crispiness.

Chicken Butter Masala

Butter chicken masala recipe or butter chicken recipe is a spectacular chicken curry recipe which is crazily popular all over the world. It is one of the most ordered chicken curries in Indian restaurants worldwide. Butter chicken is a rich, creamy, lip smacking chicken curry where yogurt marinated subtle chicken tikkas are dunked in buttery smooth tomato gravy.
